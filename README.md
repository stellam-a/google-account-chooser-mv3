# Quick Account Switcher for Google Accounts

Have you ever wanted to check your personal Gmail in one browser tab while keeping your school or work YouTube channel open in another? Normally, changing your Google account switches it across your entire browser. 

This extension solves that problem! It lets you set a **different Google account for each individual browser tab**, so you can multi-task seamlessly without constantly logging in and out.

---

## 🛠️ How to Install (Step-by-Step)

Because this is an independent open-source project, you will install it directly into your browser manually. Don't worry—it takes less than two minutes and requires no technical skills!

### Step 1: Download the Files
1. Scroll up to the top of this GitHub page.
2. Click the green button that says **Code** (located near the top right).
3. In the drop-down menu that appears, click **Download ZIP**.
4. This will download a file named something like `Google-Account-Chooser-Extension-master.zip` to your computer.

### Step 2: Unzip the Folder
1. Go to your computer's **Downloads** folder.
2. Right-click on the downloaded `.zip` file.
3. Select **Extract All...** (Windows) or double-click the file (Mac) to unzip it.
4. This creates a normal, unzipped folder. Keep this folder open or remember where it is!

### Step 3: Open Chrome's Extension Page
1. Open your Google Chrome browser.
2. In the web address bar at the very top, type **`chrome://extensions/`** and press **Enter**.
3. You will see a page displaying all your currently installed extensions.

### Step 4: Turn on Developer Mode
1. Look at the **top right corner** of the Extensions page.
2. Find the toggle switch labeled **Developer mode**.
3. **Turn it ON** (the switch will turn blue). Don't worry, this is completely safe and simply lets you use your downloaded folder.

### Step 5: Load the Extension
1. Look at the **top left corner** of the page and click the button that says **Load unpacked**.
2. A window will pop up asking you to select a folder.
3. Find and click on the **unzipped folder** you extracted during *Step 2*.
4. Click **Select Folder** (or **Open**).

🎉 **You are all set!** The extension icon will now appear in your browser.

---

## 🚀 How to Use It

1. Click the **puzzle piece icon** 🧩 in the top-right corner of your Chrome browser window to open your extensions menu.
2. Find **Google Account Chooser** and click the **pin icon** next to it so it stays visible on your toolbar.
3. Navigate to any Google site (like YouTube, Gmail, or Google Drive).
4. Click the extension icon. A beautiful, dark-themed menu will drop down showing all the Google accounts you are logged into.
5. Click on the profile you want to use for **this specific tab**, and the page will instantly refresh using that account!

* **100% Private:** This extension runs entirely on your local machine. It does not track your data, collect passwords, or send your information to any external servers.
